What is Tag Game?

Developed by JetGames, Tag Game is a fun local multiplayer online game where two to up to four players chase, catch, and tag each other on different maps. One of the players is the tagger, and their objective is to chase and tag others. When a player is tagged by the tagger, they immediately become the new tagger, and the game continues. When time is up, the tagger loses the game. The game has 3 distinct maps, each presenting different environments and challenges.

How to Play Tag Game: Step-by-step Instructions

Set the Game

Before the chase begins, take a few moments to set the game to match your playstyle. From the main menu, select the number of players: 2, 3, or 4. Each player is assigned a distinct color: red, blue, yellow, or green.

Next, choose a map of your preference. Press “Game Settings” to reveal other settings options, where you can turn “Buffs” on/off and select the time length for the match (60, 120, or 180 seconds).

Enter the Tag Game Match: Chase and Catch

When you are ready, join the match. Run, chase, tag, and doge using speed, jumps, and obstacles to escape. If you are not the tagger, stay away from the tagged player to avoid getting caught. If you are the tagger, chase at your full speed while finding ways and strategies to tag one another; try to close the distance and tag them, then run away.

Use Shortcuts

Jumping on bounce pads and using teleporters to escape or catch others. With bounce pads or trampolines, you can get launched high into the air and reach higher platforms easily. Meanwhile, teleporters instantly transport you to another part of the map for quick navigation. This can be a lifesaver when you’re a bit away from being tagged or a smart way to chase and surprise other players.

Pay Attention to the Countdown Timer

If time is running out and you're the tagger, rush to chase and tag someone quickly to avoid losing the game. On the other hand, if you're avoiding being tagged, use the remaining time to maintain a safe distance from the “tagger” and secure your position as the winner.

Game Controls

  • Player 1 (Red): WAD
  • Player 2 (Blue): Arrow keys
  • Player 3 (Yellow): IJL
  • Player 4 (Green): TFH
  • Pause the game: Esc

4 Best Tips and Tricks to Avoid Getting Caught

Keep moving. It’s not a good idea to remain in one place for too long since this makes you an easy target. Stay on the run, dodge, jump, and change directions to make it harder for the tagger to predict your movements.

Use quick turns and shortcuts. This makes it more challenging to catch up, especially if the chaser is right behind you. Do not just run in a straight line. Instead, switch directions unexpectedly to surprise the tagger.

Use the environment and take advantage of your surroundings. Such as hiding behind trees and climbing the structures to avoid getting tagged. Obviously, getting to higher positions takes longer for the chaser to reach you, while hiding behind big trees makes you invisible from the chaser’s sight.

Keep an eye on the tagger’s movements. Knowing where they are helps you plan your next move and avoid running straight into them. If the tagger is focusing on another player, use the opportunity to reposition yourself in a safer spot.

FAQs

Do different characters have unique abilities?

No, all players typically have the same abilities regardless of color—no one has an advantage over others in the Tag game online. Success depends on the player’s skills and strategies. Most importantly, the game offers endless fun for everyone.

Can I play Tag Game unblocked with a CPU?

No. The game doesn’t offer any mode that allows you to play with a CPU. It requires at least 2 players playing on the same PC to enjoy the game.

How to win in Tag Game online?

To be the winner, avoid being the tagger when time runs out. If you're still tagged when time is up, you lose!
